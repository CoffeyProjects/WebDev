Artists of the Float or Flight

Sammy M.

Sammy M. is one of the most decorated artists in the industry. She loves doing face painting and making jewlery like bracelets for little girls to wear. She'll be at the event waiting for any who would dare witness her beautiful art.

Matt Johnson

Matt Johnson is another warior to the world of arts. He does a lot of portrait paintings and they looks absolutely amazing! He's won two Oscars twice in a single year for art he's does over 12 years ago. Go see him!


Trenton Brenton

Trenton Brenton is mostly known for his sculpting. He has sculped many beautiful pieces like the Bone-o-Lisa and the Linking Hand sculptures for example. He will be waiting for you all in the booths! Enjoy the art!

Larry L. Berry

Larry Berry is mostly known threw his comic book illustrations. He has worked on over 20 comic book series' in a years, which is a pretty high feat. You already know he'll have comics at the event! Happy reading!
